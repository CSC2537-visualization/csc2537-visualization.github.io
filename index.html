Purpose

Visual perception...

This course will study techniques and algorithms for creating effective visualizations based on principles from graphic design, visual art, perceptual psychology, and cognitive science. The course is targeted both towards students interested in using visualization in their own work, as well as students interested in building better visualization tools and systems. Students will conduct reading of scientific research papers, and will lead a presentation and discussions for at least one article during the term.
